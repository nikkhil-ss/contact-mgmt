Steps to operate the web-app

1: Click and open the Link
2:Click on the contact details to get the contact details
3:Click on the "create contact" to create new contact
4:Click on the "edit" button to edit and update the existing contact.
5: Click on the "Delete" button to delete the contact.
6:Click on the left corner "contact app" text too return to the homePage.
7: On homePage click on the "Charts and Maps" to preview the graphs of the covid cases.
